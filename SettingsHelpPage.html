Press the search icon on the navigation bar. You can add friends through the suggested users shown on this page, or you can search specific users in the search bar.

Press the profile icon on the navigation bar. On the left side of the profile page, there will be a label that say "My Map." Press it, and your map along with any locations that have been saved by you will appear.

Press the profile icon on the navigation bar. On the right side of the profile page, there is a label that says "My Reviews." A list of your reviews will appear.

Press the profile icon on the navigation bar. On the right side of the profile page, there is a label that says "My Reviews." Press the + icon to write a new review.

Yes, you can edit your profile. Press the profile icon on the navigation bar. Then press the Settings icon located at the top right corner of the page. You will be redirected to the Edit Profile page.

On the right side of another user's profile, their map will appear. Under the map, there is a "Save" button, which will allow their map to be easily accessed on your own profile under "My Map".

On your profile, there is a "My Map" label. Your Map will indicate locations saved by you with a red pin, and any locations from other users' maps will come up as different color pin.

You are User A. You want to delete User B's map from your map. Press the profile icon on the navigation bar and go to "My Map". Any location saved by you will show up as a red pin on your Map, and locations from other users' maps show up as different color pins. User B appears as a blue pin on your map. Press any blue pin on your map, so a pop-up will show up with the location's information and a link to User B's profile. Under User B's map, there is an X icon, which allows the map to be deleted from your Map.

Under your map, there are sharing icons that will allow you to post your map to any social media platforms you have connected to Food Pin.
